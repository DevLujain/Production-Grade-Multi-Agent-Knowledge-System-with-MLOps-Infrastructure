# IT POLICIES

## 1. Password Security
- Minimum 12 characters
- Change every 90 days
- Never share passwords

## 2. Email Usage
Use company email for official business only.
Personal use is not permitted.

## 3. Software Licenses
- All software must be licensed
- Piracy is prohibited
- Report unlicensed software to IT

## 4. Backup and Recovery
All work must be saved to company servers.
Personal devices require encryption.

## 5. VPN Usage
Use VPN when working remotely.
VPN credentials are personal and non-transferable.
