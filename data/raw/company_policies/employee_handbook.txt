# EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

## 1. Code of Conduct
All employees must follow company policies and treat colleagues with respect.

## 2. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday.
Flexible hours available upon manager approval.

## 3. Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 25 days
- Sick Leave: 5 days per year
- Maternity Leave: 16 weeks paid

## 4. Data Protection
All employee data is protected under GDPR.
Confidential information must not be shared.

## 5. Remote Work
Employees can work remotely 2 days per week.
Must maintain productivity and communication.

## 6. Training and Development
Company provides budget for professional development.
Minimum 2 training days per year.

## 7. Grievance Procedure
- Step 1: Talk to direct manager
- Step 2: HR department
- Step 3: Formal hearing
